Posted by Rorie Hinds

Salt Lake City, Utah Asset Management Division – Public Investing Internship Dear Recruiting Team, As a Pre-Business (applying to finance this summer) student at Brigham Young University with a strong interest in markets and a growing foundation in investment management, I’m eager to contribute to and learn from one of the most respected teams in the industry. After attending a recent Women at Goldman Sachs networking event, I came away even more inspired by the firm’s inclusive culture and the incredible people driving its success. My experience so far has given me a unique perspective on both sides of finance. I started at an advertising firm managing corporate budgets, where I learned to think critically about how businesses allocate resources. This summer, I’ll be interning at Cairn Wealth Advisory, where I’ll help craft investment strategies for high-net-worth clients. These opportunities have fueled my interest in asset management—especially the challenge of making thoughtful, data-driven decisions in dynamic public markets. Outside of class, I lead the Personal Finance Association at BYU, a student organization I helped launch to make financial literacy more accessible. Our first event brought together 60+ students with a campus tax consultant, and it confirmed something I’ve come to believe: when finance is made approachable, it’s incredibly empowering.
